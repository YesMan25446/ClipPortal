Submit a Clip

Share a link to your clip or upload a file. Provide a title and short description. Maximum length is 30 seconds.

YouTube, Twitch, or direct .mp4 links work best.
Max length 30 seconds (files >30s will be rejected). ~100MB recommended.

Submission Guidelines

  • Include a clear, descriptive title.
  • Prefer links to well-known platforms (YouTube, Twitch).
  • Only upload content you have the right to share.
  • Keep descriptions concise and helpful.
  • Choose the most appropriate category for your clip.
  • Ensure your clip is 30 seconds or less.
